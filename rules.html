Rules

Dear participants, Here are some very important rules and regulations that you need to follow for Hackerrupt'20

  1. Please bring your college ID card which is a must. You will not be permitted in without your ID card.
  2. WiFi will be provided but you can also bring your own dongle or use your own internet.
  3. Participants are requested to bring their own laptops, chargers, pendrives,hardware components and other accessories. We advice you take care of them and the ACE team will not be responsible for your personal belongings.
  4. Participants are requested to pre install all their required softwares as heavy downloading of large files will not be possible for all of them at the same time.
  5. You can also bring blankets or pillows in case it gets cold or you need to take a nap.
  6. Also please note the dress code for the hackathon-
    • Boys: Shorts are not allowed
    • Girls: Sleeveless clothings are not allowed
    • Please ensure that you follow the dress code and avoid inappropriate dressing.
  7. All teams are requested to start their projects strictly at the venue and during the hackathon.
    Also teams violating our rules and indulging in other unprofessional behaviour or activities will be disqualified on the spot without question.
  8. Another important thing we'd like to stress is that- With the current outbreak of coronavirus (COVID- 19) to ensure your safety while you travel we are sharing some of the guidelines to ensure your safe travel.
    • We insist that you stay at home if you feel unwell, your health is more important than any other thing.
    • We also advise you to stay at home if you begin to feel unwell, even with mild symptoms such as headache and a slight runny nose, until you recover.
    • You can also carry all sanitary items you might require such as sanitizers and wet wipes to ensure protection.

We hope you have a safe journey, looking forward to meeting you at Hackerrupt'20 on 14th and 15th March!

With regards,

ACE team
