Domains

  • Medtech/ Biotech/ Healthtech

    Medical technology (MedTech) refers to all kinds of medical products that can be used for the treatment of various medical conditions and diseases. Advancements in this field are designed primarily to improve the overall quality of medical care and cutting edge technology continues to be a demand in the industry with the growing population provides a variety of chances for inventiveness.

  • Agriculture, Foodtech and Rural Development

    Food technology is the application of food science to the selection, preservation, processing, packaging, distribution and use of safe food. We need to develop solutions while keeping in mind the necessity to manage and process our agricultural output as well as improve India's rural section.

  • Transportation and logistics

    Logistics involves the management of the inward and outward transportation of goods from the manufacturer to the end user. Transportation involves the movement of goods or people. Being a important part of our day to day life, suggestions which help to relieve the increasing demands on the city's resources, transportation systems, and logistical infrastructure while also developing an innovative solution are appreciated.

  • Renewable/ Sustainable energy

    Renewable energy is generated by sources that can be replenished within a relatively short period of time. The use of various computer technologies in this area could contribute to the modernization of the renewable energy industry and thus lead to huge improvements in this sector.

  • Blockchain and Cybersecurity

    A blockchain is a distributed database or ledger that is shared among the nodes of a computer network while cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. Both these domains aim to provide security and transparency thus suggesting solutions involving decentralized, distributed ledger systems that enables cryptocurrencies and NFTs and has the potential to drastically alter many industries.

  • Fintech (Financial Technology)

    FinTech (financial technology) is a catch-all term referring to software, mobile applications, and other technologies created to improve and automate traditional forms of finance for businesses and consumers alike. Fintech is a crucial domain which deals with the way the companies move, store, and safeguard digital money and thus advancements in technology can develop this field.

  • Education

    Smart education is a term used to define education in the digital age. It makes it possible for students to learn more effectively, quickly, flexibly, and comfortably.In the modern day, we observe several developments in this field that have transformed education via the employment of various computer technologies.

  • Disaster Management

    Risk reduction, planning, and management concepts are all part of disaster management, whether it be before, after, or during a disaster. The damage resulting from such calamities can be highly expensive and heavy, so in the modern world, solutions that can reduce or avoid the damage are crucial.

  • National Security

    National security is a crucial subject that needs to be managed carefully. Computers play a significant part in national defence and military strategy, thus any ideas that help increase national security are essential to preventing cyberattacks.

  • Fitness and Sports

    Technologies have been developed in the contemporary era to track different sports parameters and help people get fitter. There is a need for new developments in this area that can promote fitness activities, help people stay fit, or monitor their performance.

  • IoT(Internet of Things)

    The Internet of Things (IoT) describes the network of physical objects—“things”—that are embedded with sensors, software, and other technologies for the purpose of connecting and exchanging data with other devices and systems over the internet.Future market dominance will come from the IoT sector, and it will be fascinating to look into any developments or breakthroughs in this area by the participants.
